F1

The world Formula 1 Championship (F1) is the highest level annual series of field racing competitions held by the International Federation of motor sports (FIA). It is the highest level of racing competition in the world. It is called "three major sports events in the world" together with the Olympic Games and the world cup. The first race was held at Silverstone in 1950.
